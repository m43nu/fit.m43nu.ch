This is the last Workout published on this Blog.<br/>

**Hero WOD: Murph**
* 1.6KM Run
* 100 Pull-Ups - [Strict](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HRV5YKKaeVw), [Kipping](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lzRo-4pq_AY) or [Butterfly](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OenVG15QMj8)
* 200 [Push-Ups](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_l3ySVKYVJ8) (lower thighs and chest touch the floor)
* 300 [Air Squats](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C_VtOYc6j5c) (below parallel)
* 1.6KM Run

*Notes:*<br/>
All with a weighted Vest 9kg for Men, 6kg for Women.<br/>
I suggest to do the Pull-Ups either Kipping or Butterfly.

**Scaling options:**
* no weighted Vest
* divide the Reps: 20x: 5 Pulls-Ups, 10 Push-Ups and 15 Air Squats
* Pull-Ups: [Use a Band](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-njdV6oQb7o) or replace it with [Bent Over Rows](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pyBHPoC8Nzo)
