We have the "Bergeron Beep Test" by [COMPTRAIN](https://comptrain.co/home-gym/).

**METCON: Every Minute on the Minute (EMOM)**
* 7 [Goblet Thrusters (single DB)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ib-kaOjO4Tg) OR [Odd Object Thrusters](https://youtu.be/qsHkVCkeab0)
* 7 [Single Arm Alternating DB Power Snatches](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9520DJiFmvE) OR [Odd Object Ground to Overhead](https://youtu.be/kRsckQhX0is)
* 7 Burpees

*Notes:*<br/>
You repeat these 3 exercises at every start of the minute until you can't make it within that minute anymore.<br/>
After 10 rounds, add (1) rep to each movement.

**CORE: Tabata [Side Planks](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IkMmABQ9SkM)**<br/>
**8 Rounds:**<br/>
* 20 Seconds On
* 10 Seconds Rest

**Have fun :)**
