AMRAP from [COMPTRAIN](https://comptrain.co/home-gym/), Core from the one and only [Durante](https://www.instagram.com/davedurante).

**[AMRAP](https://www.yourdictionary.com/amrap) 15min "Farm Land"**<br/>
9 strict Pullups<br/>
15 [Hand Release Push-Ups](https://images.app.goo.gl/ToznrytiroJtHNSM7)<br/>
21 [Kettlebell/Dumbbell Swings](https://images.app.goo.gl/8eZdhM2U8BieW6p88)<br/>
*Notes*<br/>
Pullups can be replaced with [banded pulldowns](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=erv3HGdNMFI) or any [other replacement](https://wepullup.com/pull-up-alternatives/).<br/>
Kettlebell/Dumbbell can be replaced with any object that has a certain weight (loaded Backpack, Books, Sixpack Water/Beer, your Dog, ...)<br/>
I used a 22.5kg Dumbbell<br/>

**Durante CORE: 5 Rounds**<br/>
10 Hollow Rocks<br/>
10 V-Ups<br/>
10 Tuck Ups<br/>
10 second Hollow Hold<br/>
1 minute Rest<br/>
*Notes*<br/>
If you can't do V-Ups, double the Tuck Ups to 20.<br/>

**Stay healthy (mind and body)!**
