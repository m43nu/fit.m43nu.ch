Again a traditional Crossfit training day.

**STRENGTH**<br/>
* 3x5 [Backsquats](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ultWZbUMPL8) @ 60% of 1RM (1 Repetition Max)
* 3x5 [Frontsquats](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m4ytaCJZpl0) @ 60% of 1RM

*Notes*<br/>
If you don't know your 1 Repetition Max, start at 1/3 of your bodyweight and go up if it's too easy.

**NO EQUIPMENT ALTERNATIVE**<br/>
[Bring Sally Up With Squats](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OBLvsBfI5Ks)

**METCON: Annie's Kickbox DT**<br/>
50 - 40 - 30 - 20 - 10<br/>
[Sit-Ups](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIZX2Ru9qU8)<br/>
Before every round "Kickbox DT":<br/>
12 [Down-Ups](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nEQLZPPB0Ek)<br/>
9 [Squat-Kicks](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bPsjdXuSx48)<br/>
6 Jab + Punch + Round alternating side

*Notes*<br/>
Workout goes: 12 Down-Ups, 9 Squat Kicks, 6 JPR and 50 Sit-Ups.<br/>
Then: 12 Down-Ups, 9 Squat Kicks, 6 JPR and 40 Sit-Ups, etc.

**Lets keep or build some muscles.**
