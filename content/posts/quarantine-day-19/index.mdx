An easy going 30min Recovery Workout.

**40sec ON / 20sec OFF for 30min**<br/>
* Jump-Rope or [Jumping Jacks](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SfaNwjsK0Lo)
* [Inchworms](https://youtu.be/VSp0z7Mp5IU?t=20)
* [Air Squats](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C_VtOYc6j5c)
* [Starfish V-Ups](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZDxqMzMLkRc) -> change to [Arch Hold](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=prz7jmsyOaQ) after 3 Rounds
* [Straddle Stretch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r15VoakKG-E)

*Notes*<br/>
Workout goes: Set a timer with 40seconds work and 20seconds rest for 30 rounds or just a timer with 30mins.<br/>
You start with 40secs Jump-Rope/Jumping-Jacks then 20secs rest, 40secs Inchworms then 20secs rest, etc.

**Recover and relax :)**
