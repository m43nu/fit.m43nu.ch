Strength for your legs, and a nice Metcon from COMPTRAIN.

**STRENGTH**<br/>
* 3x3 [Backsquats](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ultWZbUMPL8) @ 80% of 1RM (1 Repetition Max)
* 3x3 [Frontsquats](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m4ytaCJZpl0) @ 80% of 1RM

*Notes*<br/>
If you don't know your 1 Repetition Max, start at 1/3 of your bodyweight and go up if it's too easy.

**NO EQUIPMENT ALTERNATIVE**<br/>
[Bring Sally Up With Squats](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OBLvsBfI5Ks)

**METCON: Castaway - AMRAP 20min**<br/>
* 4 Strict Pull-ups
* 8 Burpees
* 12 [Single Dumbbell Step Back Lunges](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IUdZp0QUOc0)

**No Equipment Version: AMRAP 20min**<br/>
* 8 [Odd-Object Rows](https://youtu.be/5bTX5qKWtEA)
* 8 Burpees
* 12 [Odd-Object Step-Back Lunges](https://youtu.be/Lc3gBYXT2lI)
